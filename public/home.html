Our Food

At Da Gurkha, we celebrate Nepal’s culinary heritage with time-honored family recipes. From hand-folded momos to slow-cooked Himalayan curries, every dish reflects our traditions. Experience the authentic taste of Nepal.

Who Are We?

When Da Gurkha’s founder moved from Nepal to Liverpool in [year], he longed for the flavors of home. Finding nowhere to enjoy authentic Nepalese cuisine, he opened one of the city’s first Nepalese restaurants. Rooted in tradition and warm hospitality, Da Gurkha brings the taste of the Himalayas to Liverpool—where every dish tells a story of home.
