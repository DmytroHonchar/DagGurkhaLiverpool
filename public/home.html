Our Team

At Da Gurkha, we believe in real, honest cooking. Our founder grew up making Nepalese food the traditional way. When he moved to Liverpool, he missed the flavors of home, so he decided to bring a piece of Nepal here. Today, our team continues his passion, using fresh ingredients and classic recipes to share the true taste of Nepal with everyone.

About Da Gurkha

Da Gurkha is a place to experience real Nepalese food in a warm and welcoming setting. We use fresh ingredients and traditional recipes to bring out the best flavors in every dish. It’s authentic, flavorful and made with care, just like home. Whether you come with friends, for a special date, or just to enjoy a meal on your own, you'll always feel welcome here.
