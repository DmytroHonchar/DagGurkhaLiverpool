Our Food

At Da Gurkha, we celebrate Nepal’s culinary heritage with time-honored family recipes. From hand-folded momos to slow-cooked Himalayan curries, every dish reflects our traditions. Experience the authentic taste of Nepal.

Explore Menu

Our Team

At Da Gurkha, our approach is simple. Our founder grew up learning real Nepalese recipes, and he built this restaurant on a foundation of careful preparation and respect for tradition. Today, our team works together to prepare each dish with precision and care—using quality ingredients and techniques that have been refined over time.

About Da Gurkha

Our restaurant is designed to let the food speak for itself. We offer a menu of Nepalese dishes prepared using time-tested methods and fresh, carefully selected ingredients. With a straightforward setting and an emphasis on honest cooking, Da Gurkha is a place where you can enjoy well-prepared food in a relaxed atmosphere.
