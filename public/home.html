Who Are We?

Da Gurkha is a family-owned Nepalese restaurant in Liverpool, bringing authentic Himalayan cooking to our community. Every dish—from our famous momos to our aromatic curries—reflects generations of tradition and the warmth of Nepalese hospitality. Come taste the vibrant flavors of the Himalayas right here in Liverpool.

Our Flavors

At Da Gurkha, we honor Nepal's rich culinary heritage through family recipes passed down generations. From hand-folded momos steamed to perfection to aromatic curries slow-cooked with Himalayan spices, each dish tells a story of our mountains. Join us for an authentic taste of Nepal's soul-warming cuisine.
